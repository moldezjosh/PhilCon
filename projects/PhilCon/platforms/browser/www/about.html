About 1987 PhilCon

1987 PhilCon is an offiline reference for the 1987 Contitution, The Constitution of the Republic of the Philippines. It contains all the articles (18 Articles) of the 1987 Constitution.

A mobile app that can help those students who are studying the constitution.
